Setup and Delivery

Setup:

We make the process fast and easy for you. When you have been confirmed for an appointment, we send you an e-mail with the invoice, a video on how to prepare for the shoot, and questions for 3D Virtual Tour customization.

Delivery:

We deliver your Matterport Real Estate Bundle within 3 business days (standard), or within 24 hours after the shoot if you ordered expedited delivery. It is delivered by e-mail with instructions under each link on how to use it and where to place it, as well as a Google Drive folder containing all the photos. Your receipt is attached.
